General Rules For The Student

  • 1. Students must attend the school regularly.
  • 2. Students must keep their books & copies clean & tidy.
  • 3. chool hours given in the Class routine must be strictly followed.
  • 4. Wearing school inform in the school is compulsory for all students.
  • 5. Parents/Guardians are requested to encourage their children to speak in English at home.
  • 6. The School Diary must be cheeked and Signed by the parents regularly.
  • 7. Parents/Guardians should discuss classroom activities & other problems with concern teachers.
  • 8. Parents/Guardians should not enter the classroom other than the Parentâ€™s day.
  • 9. Parents/Guardians are the first proper educator of their children. The School will guarantee of success & progress in education when full co-operation is given by them.
  • 10. Annual fees/Charges are payable in one installments as prescribed by the school on or before the scheduled dates.
  • 11. Fees once paid are not transferable/adjustable or refundable under any circumstances.
  • 12. Parents will be expected to pay separately to the school for outstation tours, Entry fees for various competitions and fees for Registration/internal/Public/Board exams as applicable from time to time.
  • 13. Parents should expect a hike percentage in the annual fees every year.
  • 14. Transport Fess (if opted for) will be payable term wise only. Service Tax as applicable will be payable.
  • 15. Allotment of shifts & divisions will be at the sole discretion of the school Administrative Department.
  • 16. Any Certificate will be issued after one week of application. Tk. 1000/- will be charged for such certificates.
  • 17. Duplicate ID card will be provided @ Tk. 300/- after 15 days of receipt of such payment.
  • 18. If admission is cancelled in the midterm, No Dues Certificate and Transfer Certificate will be issued only after clearance of the fees for the whole year.
  • 19. School Authority/Board of Trustees reserves the right to modify the fees from time to time.
