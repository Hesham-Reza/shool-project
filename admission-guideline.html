Admission Guideline

Admission
  • 1. One who will be responsible to the Principal for the child must introduce every candidate for admission in person.
  • 2. New admission will be made subject to availability of seats.
  • 3. Students coming from recognized school will not be admitted without the original Transfer Certificate (TC) from that school.
  • 4. If they come from a recognized school outside, they shall have their Transfer Certificate counter signed by the District Education Officer (DEO) regarding concerned area.
  • 5. Pupils will be given admission only through proper channels. No special reference or recommendation will be entertained or considered.
  • 6. The school management or Board of Trustees reserves the right to admit or retain students in the school.
Conversion Form

All students from Playgroup to Class nine will have to fill a conversion form in January & raise an Invoice for the next academic year in the month of December for admission to the next class for the next academic year. This will ensure natural promotion to the next class subject to the rules & regulations of the school. However, promotion will be subject to the academic performance, attendance and other general rules & regulations of the school. It will be assumed that a parent is not interested in taking admission for the next academic year if the conversion form and Invoice are not raised on or before the last date. These seats will then filled by fresh applications. No application will be entertained for conversion after the last date.
